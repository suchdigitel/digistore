Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Digistore collects only the minimum information required to confirm purchases and deliver digital products (name, email, product code, and uploaded payment screenshot). We do not store payment credentials; payments are processed via UPI apps or payment gateways you choose to use.

How we use your data

  • To verify payments and deliver products
  • To respond to support inquiries
  • To occasionally send product updates (only if you opt-in)

Customer Rights

  • Right to Access: You will receive access to purchased files upon verification.
  • Right to Quality Delivery: If files are corrupted, contact us within 7 days for a replacement.
  • Right to Privacy: We use your form-upload only for verification and will not share it with third parties.

Because our products are digital, refunds are limited. See our No Refund / All Sales Are Final page for full details.
